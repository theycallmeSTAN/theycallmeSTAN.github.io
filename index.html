About Yashvardhan Also known as (They Call Me Stan)

They Call Me Stan is an independent Desi hip-hop artist representing Diu Island, India. Known for emotional storytelling, raw expression, and relatable lyricism, his music reflects real-life struggles, ambition, and inner battles. Blending hardcore rap energy with vulnerable narratives, Stan creates songs that connect deeply with listeners who understand pain, growth, and self-belief.And Mainly Inspired From Mainstream Artist MC STAN.

Inspired by the evolving Indian hip-hop movement, They Call Me Stan focuses on authenticity over trends, delivering verses that are personal, intense, and unapologetically real.
